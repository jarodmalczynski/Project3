Stevie Wonder

Stevie Wonder, known as one of the most successful musical performers of the late 20th century.
A man who has somehow managed to receive both a best original song and worst original song award at seperate times.
A blind, multi-instrumental musical prodigy, Stevie Wonder has been a successful soul,
pop, R&B, jazz, and funk musician at different times.

Stevie Wonder is not only a talented musician, he is also a noted political activist.
He was appointed by the United Nations as a messenger of peace, he gained a lifetime acheivement award
from the National Civil Rights Museum, and he has earned a Presidential Medal of Freedom from Barack Obama.
